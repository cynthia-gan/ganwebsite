Hi!

I am a Master’s student and aspiring clinical psychology researcher at the University of Toronto Scarborough, where I am co-supervised by Dr. Amanda Uliaszek and Dr. Zindel Segal. I also currently work on the CALM study at SickKids.

My research interests include Cluster B personality disorders, self-harm and suicidality, and eating disorders. I hope one day to help create treatment and prevention programs for adolescents at risk of developing borderline features and eating disorders.

In my spare time, I sing opera, write poetry and fiction, and knit and crochet poorly. I hope to hear from you!

Hi!

I’m a research analyst at the Temerty Centre for Therapeutic Brain Stimulation at the Centre for Addiction and Mental Health in Toronto. I graduated from the University of Calgary’s Eating Behaviours Lab in spring 2023.

My research interests include Cluster B personality disorders, self-harm and suicidality, and eating disorders. I hope one day to develop prevention programs for adolescents at risk of developing borderline features.

In my spare time, I sing opera, write poetry and fiction, and knit poorly. I hope to hear from you!
